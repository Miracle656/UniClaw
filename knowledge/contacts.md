## How to Email a Lecturer
- Format is usually: firstname.lastname@unilag.edu.ng
- Example: john.doe@unilag.edu.ng
- Always include: your name, matric number, department, level, and course code in your email
- Subject line: [COURSE CODE] - [Your Issue] e.g. "CHM101 - Missing Result Inquiry"
- Be polite and formal — Nigerian university culture values respect

## FAQs

**Q: I have an emergency on campus — who do I call?**
A: UNILAG Security at the main gate (physically go there or call the main switchboard), then the Health Centre for medical emergencies. For life-threatening situations, call 112.

**Q: I need to speak to someone about mental health — is it confidential?**
A: Yes. The University Health Centre has counselling services. Student Affairs also provides pastoral support. It is confidential.

**Q: How do I reach my faculty if I don't know my HOD's contact?**
A: Go physically to the faculty office and speak to the Departmental Secretary. They manage all student-facing communications.
