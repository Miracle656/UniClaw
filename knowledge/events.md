## FAQs

**Q: How do I join a club or association?**
A: Show up to their meetings or events. Most clubs have open membership — just find their WhatsApp group or IG page. Some have nominal dues (₦500 – ₦2,000/session).

**Q: How do I organize an event on campus?**
A: Apply for permission through the Dean of Students Affairs office. You'll need a letter from your association and a venue request.

**Q: Where is the Student Centre?**
A: Central campus, near the main library. It's the social hub — cafeteria, SUG offices, notice boards.

**Q: How do I find my departmental WhatsApp group?**
A: Ask a senior student in your department or your course rep. Groups are usually shared during orientation.
